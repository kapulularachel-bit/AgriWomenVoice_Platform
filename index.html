Services Built Around Local Needs

Designed to bridge the digital literacy divide with our multilingual voice-first technology stack.

Microfinance & Loans

Evaluate farmer credit risks with intelligent AI analysis based on crop cycles and plot surveys, allowing local microfinance lenders to approve credit securely.

Land Plot Registration

Log crop acreage, village names, and GPS coordinate boundaries. Our extension officers review and verify data to qualify farmers for financial loans.

Voice-First IVR & USSD

No internet required. Farmers with standard non-smart feature phones call our local numbers to listen to training, ask AI questions, and register crops.

Direct Crop Marketplace

Bypass middlemen. Farmers post produce quantities, and institutional agro-buyers search, view, and send direct text-messages to complete sales.

Localized Forecasts

Daily weather forecasts specifically computed for Kasungu, Dowa, and Dedza districts with drought or flooding alerts delivered instantly.

Audio Micro-lessons

Listen to short lessons on sustainable techniques, Fall Armyworm control, organic fertilizers, and post-harvest grain dry-storage.

About AgriWomen

Our Mission: Empowering Malawi's Female Smallholders

AgriWomen is a community-driven initiative dedicated to bridging gender gaps in Malawi's agricultural sector. Through inclusive, voice-enabled financial technology, we help female farmers gain true financial autonomy, secure land titles, and sell directly to wholesale buyers.

Voice-First Support

Empowering rural farmers with low literacy by replacing complex web apps with offline voice calls (IVR) and USSD codes.

Fair Crop Loans

Replacing predatory local lenders with credit risk scoring and verified land boundaries to unlock commercial loans.

Middleman-Free Trade

Bypassing middlemen so farmers can keep 100% of profits by selling directly to institutional and national buyers.
